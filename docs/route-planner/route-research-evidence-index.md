## Evidence classes

The linked records distinguish exact binary evidence, source-audited mechanism,
extracted content, witnessed/native state, community route knowledge, and
hypothesis. These classes are not interchangeable:

- source-family code without an exact registered build cannot enable a fact;
- an encoded destination does not prove its activation contract;
- a generic component-splice capability does not establish a trick-specific
  preservation set;
- a mechanism shared across builds does not establish equal interaction
  geometry; and
- a solver fixture proves causal composition of its stated facts, not that every
  premise has been witnessed in retail play.
